Bio

I am a PhD student of Comptuer Sceince at University of Newcastle where I worked at the ARC Training Centre for Food and Beverage Supply Chain Optimisation collaborated with CSRIO and Sydney University.

My general research area is applied machine learning in construction industry focusing on modeling complex, richly-labeled relational structures, graphs, and networks for systems at all scales. Applications include optimization of concrete mix ratio, self-repair efficiency prediction and building service life evaluation.
